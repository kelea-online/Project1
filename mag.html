Spotify Wrapped - My 2024 Listening Journey

News | 6th Dec 2024 | By Kelea

It's that time of the year again — Spotify Wrapped is here! Life moves at the speed of light, and before we knew it, another year has flown by. Luckily, Spotify has been diligently keeping track of my musical journey, capturing every beat and every lyric that moved me. I'm eager to see which artists and songs defined 2024 for me and relive the musical memories that shaped my year.

Top Song

I played 8 018 songs this year. However, there's one song on top of my list which I played over, and over, and over again...

HERMÈS by DJ Clen featuring A-Reece, Maraza and Jay Jody

The music pulsed through the air at Reece Effect, a music festival celebrating the South African hip-hop artist A-Reece. It was April 2024, and I was experiencing the raw emotion of the live performance for the first time in a while. Little did I know, as I swayed and sang along, a skilled hand was already reaching into my man bag, deftly removing my phone without my notice. The thrill of the music was quickly overshadowed by the sinking realization of my loss. The joy of the moment, tainted by the sting of theft.

Yet, even in the face of this minor setback, the music held its power. It was a reminder of the resilience of the human spirit, the ability to find joy amidst adversity. The song, with its poignant lyrics and infectious rhythm, became a symbol of that resilience. And so, I turned to the song for solace immediately after it dropped, pressing play 46 times over the following weeks. Each listen was a journey back to that April evening with it performed live, a bittersweet reminder of the low of night. The music, once a source of pure joy, now carried the weight of both the memory and loss of uninsured smartphone device.

While it's not a competition... here's the leaderboard for my top 5 most played songs

  1. HERMÈS - DJ Clen featuring A-Reece, Maraza and Jay Jody
  2. BABY JACKSON - Blxckie featuring A-Reece
  3. EIGHT SUMMERS - The Big Hash
  4. Malume - Marcus Harvey featuring Sai Hle
  5. VAMONOS - Jay Jody featuring A-Reece

My 2024 Music Evolution

I've changed, and so has my listening moments

  • February was my Boujee American Football Rap(whatever that means) phase, and I played artists like Future and Travis Scott.
  • July was my Unwinding Escape Room Neo Soul season, I was all about artists like NxWorries, Marcus Harvey and Frank Oceon.
  • September was my Smooth Abstract Hip Hop moment, I was big on artists like Kendrick Lamar, Drake and Big Sean.
